# Three new languages, and the rest fully translated

WhatCable now speaks three more languages, and the parts that were quietly still in English are now properly translated everywhere.

## New languages

Dutch, Turkish, and Ukrainian. That brings WhatCable to 19 languages.

## The diagnostics are translated now

The status banners and the cable, charging, and display findings (the green and amber lines that tell you what's actually going on) were still showing in English even when the rest of the app was in your language. That affected every non-English language, not just one. They're translated now.

Thanks to @scandella, who spotted it on a French Mac and reported it.

## Reachable in Settings

A few languages shipped translations but couldn't actually be picked in Settings. Every language WhatCable ships can now be selected, and the picker keeps itself in sync from here on.

Translations are machine-assisted and reviewed for formatting and placeholders. If something reads oddly in your language, tell us and we'll fix it.
